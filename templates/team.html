Our Team Members

Terry Hu, Founding Partner

Mr. Hu is a Co-Founder and CEO of Twin Peaks Capital.

Prior to founding Twin Peaks Capital, Mr. Hu was a Co-Founder and Co-President of FountainVest Partners, a leading China focused private equity firm. During his 13 years at FountainVest, Mr. Hu led a wide range of successful investments in the consumer, technology, healthcare and media space, including a multi-billion landmark leveraged take-private buyout of Focus Media, China’s largest outdoor media company, and growth capital investment in various leading internet companies in China.

Prior to co-founding FountainVest, Mr. Hu was a Managing Director at Temasek Holdings, Singapore’s sovereign wealth fund. He was a member of Temasek’s global investment committee, and head of its real estate investment. Previously, Mr. Hu had been an investment banker with Credit Suisse First Boston and Bear Stearns in Hong Kong and Shanghai respectively for over 10 years.

Mr. Hu graduated from Fudan University in Shanghai with a Bachelor of Art Degree in English Language and Literature

Eric Chen, Founding Partner

Mr. Chen is a co-founder and partner at Twin Peaks Capital.

Prior to Twin Peaks Capital, Mr. Chen was a managing director At FountainVest Partners, a leading multi-billion China focused private equity firm. At FountainVest partners, Mr. Chen headed its investments in the Financial & Business Services sector. Mr. Chen was involved in more than billion dollar of investments during his 13 year tenor at FountainVest.

Prior to FountainVest Partners, Mr. Chen was an investment banker with Lehman Brothers and Citigroup. Previously, he was an Engineer with Micron Technology.

Mr. Chen is a native of Shanghai, China. He Graduated from National University of Singapore with Bachelor degree (First Class Honors) in Electrical Engineering in 2004.

Tim Xia, Founding Partner

Mr. Xia is a co-founder and partner at Twin Peaks Capital.

Prior to Twin Peaks Capital, Mr. Xia was a director at FountainVest Partners. Between 2011-2021, Mr. Xia was mostly involved in some of the most successful technology investments made by FountainVest Partners.

Prior to FountainVest Partners, Mr. Xia was an investment banker with China International Capital Corporation.

Mr. Xia is a native of Beijing, China. He graduated from University of International Business and Economics with double Bachelor degree in English and Finance in 2008.
